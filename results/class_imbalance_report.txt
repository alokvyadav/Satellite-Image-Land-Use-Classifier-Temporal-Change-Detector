=== Class Imbalance Downsampling Experiment ===

Downsampled Classes: Pasture and PermanentCrop (Retained only 20% training samples)

Metric                        No Mitigation       Weighted Loss Mitigation 
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Overall Val Accuracy          0.9506              0.9493                   
Overall Macro F1              0.9263              0.9275                   
Pasture F1-Score              0.7636              0.7698                   
PermanentCrop F1-Score        0.9081              0.9295                   

Analysis:
Downsampling Pasture and PermanentCrop causes a significant drop in their individual F1-scores
due to the minority class bias in standard cross-entropy loss. By applying class weights inversely
proportional to the class frequencies (Weighted Loss Mitigation), the model is penalized more heavily
for misclassifying minority class samples. This improves the recall and F1-score of the downsampled
classes, although it may lead to a minor trade-off in the overall accuracy of majority classes.
